Ethiopian Cultural Clothes and Packed Food Store

The Ethiopian Cultural Clothes and Packed Food Store specializes in traditional Ethiopian attire,

including garments such as ‘habesha kemis’, ‘netela’, wandabo, ‘bullukko’, kuta, and callee (beads).

Additionally, the store offers a variety of packed cultural foods, including honey, butter, berbere, ‘shiro’, and ‘doro’ wet.
